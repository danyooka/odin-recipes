Catsoup!

Description

If you're hungry this is what you must eat. de-lish.

Ingredients

  • a cat
  • Udon noodles (handmade ofc)
  • broth (can be instant doesn't matter)

How to prepare an excellent meal

  1. First gather you cat
  2. Chop off his balls as this is where the flavor is
  3. Your cat may become a fatality but it is important to boil your broth
  4. Add noodles
  5. Add cat!

ENJOY!
